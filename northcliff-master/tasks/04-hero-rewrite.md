# Task 04 — Rewrite hero section with specifics

**Tier:** 1
**Type:** EDIT

## Problem
Current hero copy is generic: *"A bright new luxury apartment community coming to 1220 Jones St. in Shelton, Washington, designed for modern Northwest living."*

It doesn't tell people:
- How many units
- Bedroom mix
- Key differentiators
- Why they should care now

## Goal
Replace hero with concrete, scannable copy that includes unit count, bedroom mix, location, opening date, and a clear "act now" cue.

## Recommended copy

**Headline:**
> NorthCliff Commons

**Subhead (replace current):**
> 43 new luxury apartments in Shelton, WA — 1, 2, and 3 bedrooms with private patios, garages, and Mason County quiet.

**Tagline (small text above headline):**
> Now Accepting Leasing Interest · Opening December 2027

**Primary CTA:** Join the Priority Leasing List
**Secondary CTA:** View Floor Plans

## Variations to consider
If "luxury" doesn't fit your positioning, alternatives:
- *"43 brand-new apartments in Shelton, WA…"*
- *"New construction. 43 apartments. Mason County living done right."*
- *"43 modern apartments in the heart of Mason County."*

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Unit count visible without scrolling
- [ ] Bedroom mix visible
- [ ] Opening date visible
- [ ] Two CTAs, primary visually dominant
- [ ] Reads well on a 375px wide mobile screen
- [ ] No widows/orphans on the subhead at common breakpoints
