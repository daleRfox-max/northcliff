# Task 09 — Renter-focused amenities cards

**Tier:** 2
**Type:** CREATE

## Problem
The site mentions balconies, patios, and parking in prose, but renters skim. They want to scan a list of "what do I get" benefits in 5 seconds.

## Goal
A visual grid of amenity cards with icons + short labels. Place between Residences and Floor Plans sections.

## Recommended amenity cards
*(Confirm which apply before publishing — don't claim what you don't have)*

- 🏗️ **New Construction (2027)** — Brand-new everything, no maintenance backlog
- 🌿 **Private Balconies & Patios** — Outdoor space for every unit
- 🚗 **Garage & Covered Parking** — Garage and carport options available
- 🧺 **In-Unit Laundry** — Washer/dryer included *(VERIFY)*
- 🍳 **Modern Kitchens** — Updated appliances, finishes, and layout
- 🌲 **Quiet Mason County Setting** — Residential location, away from highway noise
- 🛏️ **1, 2, and 3 Bedroom Options** — Plans to fit different households
- 🏘️ **43-Unit Community** — Small enough to feel like a neighborhood

## Layout
- 4 columns on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 (or 2 stacked) on mobile
- Each card: large icon, short label, 1-line description
- Subtle hover effect

## Icon options
- Heroicons (free, MIT licensed)
- Lucide icons (free)
- Custom SVG illustrations if you want a more premium feel

## Don't include
- Vague items like "modern living" or "great location" — be specific
- Things you can't deliver (e.g. pool, gym) unless they're confirmed in plans

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] 6–8 amenity cards displayed in a clean grid
- [ ] Each card is a real, deliverable amenity (verified with Dale before publish)
- [ ] Icons are consistent in style
- [ ] Responsive: scales gracefully to mobile
- [ ] Loads fast (SVG icons preferred over images)
