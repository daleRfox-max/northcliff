# Task 11 — About the Development / Builder section

**Tier:** 2
**Type:** CREATE

## Problem
A faceless developer feels like a risk. Most apartment communities are built by out-of-area corporations. You're not — you're a local Mason County builder doing this in your own backyard. That's a competitive advantage being completely wasted on the current site.

## Goal
A short, well-designed section telling the developer/builder story. Builds trust and creates a "we're local, we care" differentiator.

## Content outline

**Section headline:**
> Built by a Local Mason County Team

**Body copy (draft):**
> NorthCliff Commons is developed and constructed by a Mason County builder you can find on a Tuesday morning at the jobsite. As both the owner-developer and the general contractor through F&S Construction & Septic, every detail of this 43-unit community is being managed by people who live and work in Shelton.
>
> When you rent at NorthCliff Commons, you're not dealing with a property management company three states away — you're renting from people who built it, who care about how it lives, and who plan to be around long after the ribbon is cut.

**Visual elements to include:**
- Photo of Dale or the F&S team on site (optional but high-impact)
- F&S Construction logo
- "Local builder" badge or similar trust marker
- Link to F&S Construction website

**Optional credibility additions:**
- Years in business
- Other projects completed in Mason County
- "Licensed & insured in Washington State"
- Local memberships (Chamber of Commerce, etc.)

## Placement
After Amenities, before Location. Logical flow: *Here's the project → Here's what's in it → Here's who's building it → Here's where it is.*

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Section live with builder story
- [ ] At least one human photo or strong visual (logo + photo combo)
- [ ] Link to F&S Construction site
- [ ] Mobile layout reads well
- [ ] Copy is reviewed by Dale before publish
