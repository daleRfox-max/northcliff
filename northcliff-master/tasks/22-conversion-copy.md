# Task 22 — Conversion copy rewrite

**Tier:** 4
**Type:** EDIT

## Problem
Current copy reads like a brochure. It describes the building, but doesn't pull the reader toward action. Good conversion copy speaks directly to the renter's situation and motivations.

## Goal
Rewrite copy across the site to match how renters actually think and what motivates them to take action.

## Copy principles
- Speak to the renter, not about the building
- Use "you" liberally
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Specific > vague
- Action > description

## Section-by-section rewrites

### Residences section (current: "Modern apartments with a quiet Northwest point of view.")
**Replace with:**
> ### Apartment living without the apartment hassle.
> 43 brand-new homes in Shelton, designed for people who want modern living, private outdoor space, and a quiet neighborhood — without giving up the conveniences of being close to town.

### Floor Plans section (current: "Explore building and residence plans.")
**Replace with:**
> ### Pick your future home.
> Browse 1, 2, and 3 bedroom layouts across four buildings. Each plan shows kitchens, bathrooms, closets, and patio access so you can find the right fit before you call.

### Location section (current: "Set within Shelton, NorthCliff Commons brings new apartment living to a practical Mason County location...")
**Replace with:**
> ### Right in the heart of Shelton.
> Five minutes to downtown. Easy access to Highway 101. Close to grocery, schools, parks, and everything that makes Mason County feel like home — without the highway noise.

### Leasing section (current: "Ask about availability, timing, and future leasing updates.")
**Replace with:**
> ### Be first in line when the doors open.
> NorthCliff Commons opens December 2027 with 43 units across four buildings. Join the priority list to receive floor plan releases, pricing announcements, and leasing availability before the public.
>
> **[Join the Priority List]**

### Waitlist form intro
**Replace with:**
> Be among the first to receive floor plan releases, pricing updates, construction progress, and leasing availability for NorthCliff Commons.

## Microcopy upgrades

### Form button labels
- "Submit" → "Join the List"
- "Send" → "Send My Inquiry"
- "Submit Inquiry" → "Send to Jennifer"

### Form helper text
- Email field: "We'll send you monthly updates, never spam."
- Phone field: "Optional — we'll only call if you ask us to."

### Success messages
After form submit, show:
> ✓ **You're on the list.**
> Jennifer will reach out with updates as we approach the December 2027 opening. Welcome to the NorthCliff Commons priority list.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Every section heading and intro paragraph rewritten with the new voice
- [ ] All form buttons use action-oriented labels
- [ ] Success states feel personal and human
- [ ] Copy is reviewed by Dale before publish
- [ ] Reads naturally on a phone — no marketing-speak feel
