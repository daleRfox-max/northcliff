# Task 10 — FAQ section

**Tier:** 2
**Type:** CREATE

## Problem
The site doesn't answer the obvious questions a renter would ask. They have to inquire to learn anything beyond what's on the page.

## Goal
A FAQ section with accordion-style questions, each ending with a soft CTA back to the form or waitlist.

## Suggested questions and answer skeletons

**Q: When does NorthCliff Commons open?**
A: NorthCliff Commons is scheduled to open in December 2027. We're currently accepting interest list signups so you can be among the first to receive floor plan releases, pricing updates, and leasing availability.
→ *CTA: [Join the Priority List]*

**Q: Where is NorthCliff Commons located?**
A: NorthCliff Commons is at 1220 Jones St., Shelton, WA 98584 — a quiet Mason County setting with easy access to local services, Highway 101, and the surrounding Puget Sound region.
→ *CTA: [View on Map]*

**Q: How do I join the priority leasing list?**
A: Add your name, email, and bedroom preference to the form above. You'll get monthly construction updates and first access to leasing when units become available.
→ *CTA: [Join Now]*

**Q: What floor plans are available?**
A: NorthCliff Commons offers 1, 2, and 3 bedroom apartments across four buildings (A, B, C, and D), totaling 43 units. Detailed floor plans for each unit type are available in the Floor Plans section.
→ *CTA: [View Floor Plans]*

**Q: Is parking included?**
A: Yes — both garage and covered carport parking are part of the community. Specific assignments and availability will be confirmed during leasing.
→ *CTA: [View Garage Plan]*

**Q: When will pricing be announced?**
A: Pricing will be released as we approach the opening date. Joining the priority list ensures you'll be among the first to receive pricing and availability details.
→ *CTA: [Get Notified]*

**Q: Who do I contact for leasing?**
A: Jennifer Fox is your leasing contact. Reach her at 360-229-1220 or jennifer@sentrypark.com.
→ *CTA: [Contact Jennifer]*

**Q: Who is the developer/builder?**
A: NorthCliff Commons is developed and built by a local Mason County team with F&S Construction as the general contractor. You're getting a community built by people who live here.
→ *CTA: [About the Builder]*

## Layout
- Accordion (collapsed by default) — each Q tap-expands to reveal A
- All questions visible at a glance for scannability
- SEO bonus: FAQPage JSON-LD schema (see Task 13)
