# Task 14 — Page titles & meta descriptions for local search

**Tier:** 3
**Type:** EDIT

## Problem
Current title is fine but generic and won't rank well for local search queries renters actually type into Google.

## Target search queries
Build titles and metas around what people actually search:
- "apartments in Shelton WA"
- "new apartments Shelton Washington"
- "Mason County apartments for rent"
- "luxury apartments Shelton"
- "NorthCliff Commons Shelton"
- "1 bedroom apartments Shelton WA"
- "2 bedroom apartments Shelton"
- "3 bedroom apartments Shelton WA"
- "apartments near 1220 Jones St"

## Per-page title and meta recommendations

### Homepage
- **Title:** `NorthCliff Commons | New Apartments in Shelton, WA | Opening Dec 2027`
- **Meta description:** `43 new luxury apartments in Shelton, WA. 1, 2, and 3 bedrooms with private patios and garage parking. Now accepting priority leasing interest. Opening December 2027.`

### /floor-plans (Task 15)
- **Title:** `Floor Plans | NorthCliff Commons Apartments Shelton WA`
- **Meta description:** `Browse 1, 2, and 3 bedroom apartment floor plans at NorthCliff Commons in Shelton, WA. Includes garage, patio, and parking details.`

### /location (Task 15)
- **Title:** `Location | NorthCliff Commons · 1220 Jones St., Shelton WA`
- **Meta description:** `NorthCliff Commons is located at 1220 Jones St. in Shelton, WA — a quiet Mason County setting with easy access to local services and Highway 101.`

### /leasing or /contact (Task 15)
- **Title:** `Contact Leasing | NorthCliff Commons Shelton WA`
- **Meta description:** `Contact Jennifer Fox for leasing at NorthCliff Commons in Shelton, WA. Call 360-229-1220 or join the priority list for availability updates.`

### /apartments-shelton-wa (Task 15 SEO landing page)
- **Title:** `Apartments for Rent in Shelton, WA | NorthCliff Commons`
- **Meta description:** `Looking for new apartments in Shelton, WA? NorthCliff Commons offers 43 modern 1, 2, and 3 bedroom apartments with garages and private patios. Opening Dec 2027.`

### /about (Task 15)
- **Title:** `About NorthCliff Commons | Built by F&S Construction Shelton WA`
- **Meta description:** `NorthCliff Commons is developed and built by a local Mason County team led by F&S Construction. Learn about the builder behind Shelton's newest apartment community.`

## Best practices
- Title length: 50–60 characters (avoid truncation in SERPs)
- Meta description: 150–160 characters
- Include city + state in every title
- Lead with the brand or the keyword, depending on intent
- Don't repeat the same meta across pages

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Every page has a unique, keyword-optimized title
- [ ] Every page has a unique meta description
- [ ] Titles include "Shelton" and either "WA" or "Washington"
- [ ] All metas fit within character limits
- [ ] Test in Google's SERP preview tool before going live
