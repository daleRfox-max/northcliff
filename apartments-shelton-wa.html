Mason County Living

A practical new option for Shelton renters.

Finding a new apartment in Shelton can mean balancing location, parking, floor plan options, and timing. NorthCliff Commons is being built for renters who want a new home in Mason County without leaving the daily convenience of Shelton behind.

The community is planned for 43 apartments across buildings A, B, C, and D. The verified bedroom mix includes 1, 2, and 3 bedroom plans, with garage and carport parking included in the community plan.

Because opening is scheduled for December 2027, the priority list is the best way to stay close to the project. You can receive floor plan releases, construction progress, pricing announcements, and leasing availability as details become ready to share.

NorthCliff Commons is developed by Dale and built by F&S Construction & Septic LLC, a Shelton-based general contractor. That local connection matters: this is a Mason County housing project built by people close to the site and the community it will serve.
